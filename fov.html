FOV documentation

We differentiate between two different modes:

  • fov x - alters the horizontal fov of aimbot (ranges from 1 to 3840)
  • fov y - alters the vertical fov of aimbot (ranges from 1 to 2160)

This is basically the 'range' in which your aimbot will work

The higher the value, the further your crosshair can be from your target

The performance will become slower as you try to reach higher values, it shouldn't be much of a toll till around ~400 fov

Your FOV should be at '30' at most for legit cheating, the aimbot can become obvious at higher values
