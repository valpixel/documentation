Recoil control system documentation

This feature will compensate for your gun's recoil

It starts working when you hold your left mouse button, meaning it works the best with spraying weapons

Using 'rcs strength' you can adjust how much your mouse will be pulled down when you shoot; it ranges from 1 to 10

The higher the value, the harder the pull

You should not completely rely on this feature for perfect recoil control, it is only supposed to support your slightly
