FOV documentation

We differentiate between two different modes:

  • fov x - alters the horizontal shoot range of triggerbot (ranges from 1 to 15)
  • fov y - alters the vertical shoot range of triggerbot (ranges from 1 to 15)

This is basically the 'range' in which the triggerbot will work

The higher the value, the further your crosshair can be from your target, but the less accurate it will be

The lower the value, the closer your crosshair has to be to your target

We recommend that you stay in the lower values or in the middle, as that will give you the most accurate results
