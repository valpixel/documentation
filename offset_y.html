Offset Y documentation

Adjusts the final Y position of the aimbot

This feature is to be used whenever you feel like your aimbot is aiming above/below the head

  • aiming above the head? - increase the offset (e.g. from 0.00 to 2.00)
  • aiming below the head? - decrease the offset (e.g. from 0.00 to -2.00)

Decimal places are used for the most perfect adjustments, generally you would set those up only when you were to find the perfect range for your aimbot

Keep in mind, that decimal places are written as '.' not ','
