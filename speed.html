Speed documentation

We differentiate between two different modes:

  • speed x - alters the horizontal velocity of aimbot (ranges from 0.001 to 5.000)
  • speed y - alters the vertical velocity of aimbot (ranges from 0.001 to 5.000)

In general, we recommend that your horizontal speed is higher than your vertical speed

e.g.: 'speed x = 0.900'; 'speed y = 0.350'

Higher values of speed make the aimbot less controllable, and vice-versa

Higher values also make the aimbot shake, because the cheat is not able to process that fast of a movement

The decimal places allow you to make precise adjustments to make the perfect adjustments to your aimbot

Keep in mind, that decimal places are written as '.' not ','
