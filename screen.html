Screen documentation

One of the most important components of setting the cheat up; if you do not set this up correctly, the cheat will never work

The values of screen x/y must be equal to your in-game resolution NOT your desktop resolution (if they are the same, it does not really matter)

e.g. your Valorant resolution is 1600x900, but your default desktop resolution is 1920x1080. You MUST use the Valorant resolution => screen x = 1600; screen y = 900
